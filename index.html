About me

I am a syilx woman from the Penticton Indian Band. I am passionate about idigenous language and culture revitalization. I am an interdisciplinary artist with an array of mediums. My styles range from classic to contemporary. I writes fictional short stories and modern poetry. In addition I use traditional storytelling in nsyilxcen to create a captivating and unique performance. I sing and drum traditional syilx music. I use natural materials to create art such as tule mates, baskets, mocassins and beadwork.
